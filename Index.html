About

At DR FLY Consultancy, we help students build successful global careers by guiding them through every step of the study-abroad journey. From choosing the right universities to preparing for tests and securing visas, our expert team ensures a smooth and stress-free process. We offer personalized counseling, strong documentation support (SOP, LOR, essays), scholarship assistance, education loan guidance, and complete pre-departure preparation.
